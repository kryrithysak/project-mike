What is Self-Improvement?

Self-Improvement consists of activities that develop a person's capabilities and potential, build human capital, facilitate employability, and enhance quality of life and the realization of dreams and aspirations.

There are a lot of way you can improve yourself, and here are some of the most common ones:

  • Reading
  • Workout
  • Learning a New Language
  • Journaling
  • Making New Friends
